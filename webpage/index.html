Delicious Vegan Recipes

Healthy, sustainable, and cruelty-free meals.

Avocado Toast

Ingredients: Avocado, bread, lemon, salt, pepper

Instructions: 1. Toast the bread. 2. Mash the avocado and mix with lemon, salt, and pepper. 3. Spread on toast and enjoy!

Vegan Stir-Fry

Ingredients: Tofu, soy sauce, garlic, vegetables (broccoli, carrots, peppers)

Instructions: 1. Stir-fry tofu until golden. 2. Add vegetables and cook until tender. 3. Add soy sauce and garlic, stir, and serve with rice.

Chickpea Salad

Ingredients: Chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, red onion, olive oil, lemon juice

Instructions: 1. Mix chickpeas, cucumber, tomatoes, and red onion in a bowl. 2. Drizzle with olive oil and lemon juice. 3. Season with salt and pepper, then toss and serve.
